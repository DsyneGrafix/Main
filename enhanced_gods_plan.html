The Great Paradox

At the heart of theology lies the enduring tension between God's absolute control and human freedom. This paradox is not necessarily a contradiction to be solved, but a mystery to be held in balance.

The Problem of Evil

How can a good and powerful God allow suffering? Theology suggests that trials are not random but are woven into God's plan for a greater purpose, such as spiritual growth, purification, and conforming believers to the image of Christ, even when the reasons are beyond our full comprehension.
