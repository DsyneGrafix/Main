1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and any other information you provide when contacting us or signing up for our resources
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile information if you create an account
  • Purchase Information: Billing address and payment confirmation (we do NOT store credit card details)
  • Communications: Any messages, feedback, or correspondence you send to us
  • Study Progress: Information about your progress through our educational materials (if you choose to save it)

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address (country/region level only)
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to enhance your experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our biblical education services
  • Process your purchases and deliver digital content
  • Send you updates about your orders or account
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Send educational content and resources (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and educational materials
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security
  • Comply with legal obligations
Important: We will NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate our website (e.g., email services, hosting providers, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to respond to legal process
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of AnchorStack, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition (your information would remain protected under this policy)
  • With Your Consent: With your explicit permission for any other purpose

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve our website functionality

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit some website features.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services. We use the following third-party services:

  • Payment Processing: PayPal, Stripe (they handle payment information securely)

  • Content Delivery: For hosting and delivering educational materials

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies when you visit their sites.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and hosting environments
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information (only authorized personnel)
  • Regular backups and data protection protocols

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for specific uses of your information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information.

European Users

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR. Our legal basis for processing your information is typically consent or legitimate interests.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
