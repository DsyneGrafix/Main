Why Study Satan and Evil?

Understanding the biblical concept of evil and Satan's role is crucial for comprehensive biblical literacy. From the Hebrew word "satan" meaning "adversary" to the Greek "diabolos" meaning "slanderer," Scripture provides a complex theological framework for understanding evil, temptation, and spiritual warfare. These studies examine Satan not as a sensationalized figure, but through careful biblical, historical, and theological analysis.

Far from promoting fear or fascination with evil, these studies equip believers to understand Scripture's teachings about spiritual opposition, the nature of temptation, and God's ultimate victory over darkness. By examining the biblical text in its original languages and historical contexts, we gain clarity about these often misunderstood passages.
