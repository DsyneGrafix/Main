Understanding the enemy's tactics is crucial for effective spiritual warfare. Click on a scheme to see which armor pieces are its primary counters.

Division

Deception

Undermining confidence in God's love and forgiveness by reminding of past sins.

Consuming time and energy with worldly pursuits to cause spiritual neglect.
