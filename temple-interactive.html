The Second Temple (516 BCE - 70 CE)

After 70 years of exile, a remnant returned to rebuild. Though smaller and less ornate, it represented hope and restoration.

Rebuilding Timeline

536 BCE: Foundation laid amid weeping and joy
520 BCE: Work resumes under Haggai & Zechariah
516 BCE: Temple completed and dedicated
